Supplementary Data for arXiv:2311:zzzzz

User Guide

Here, we provide supplementary data to understand results in arXiv:2311:zzzzz.

  • Cell decomposition: Cell decompositions of fundamental domains in magnetic space groups without and with particle-hole symmetry, i.e., in normal conducting phases and superconducting phases, respectively.
  • Irreducible representations: Irreducible representations and their EAZ symmetry classes in magnetic space groups without and with particle-hole symmetry.
  • Matrices for invariants: Information about topological invariants (See Secs. II B, IV B 5, and Appendix A for their definitions)

Seishiro Ono* and Ken Shiozaki*, "Towards complete characterization of topological insulators and superconductors: A systematic construction of topological invariants based on Atiyah-Hirzebruch spectral sequence", arXiv:2311.zzzzz.

(*The authors contributed equally)
